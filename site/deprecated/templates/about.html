About GWAS Archive

We have built an information extraction system called GWAS Archive that parses the biomedical literature to automatically extract associations between disease and genomic mutations that have been discovered via genome-wide association studies (GWAS) of large case/control cohorts. The resulting database has been made accessible through this interactive website.
